Brochure

Sacred Heart

Fundraising brochure for Sacred Heart Cathedral Preparatory school in San Francisco, California.

At Strongrrl, we believe graphic design is all about communication. And as communicators, we know how important it is not just to hear, but to listen and to understand your business needs. That’s when we learn about your immediate objectives, your long-term goals, your blue-sky aspirations. That’s when we begin the thinking and analysis that will lead to communications solutions that ring true to you and to those you wish to reach.

Effective visual design must go beyond cookie-cutter concepts. By entering into a dialog about your communication targets, we can develop cost-effective design solutions finely tuned to your needs and your budget. We have experience working with large corporations and new start-ups, major institutions and small non-profits. We can produce a single piece or a fully integrated print program. In every case, we will develop a design that reflects the special character of your organization and that makes the best use of your investment.

As a small but energetic firm, we can move decisively to meet your design needs, with consistently high quality and at exceptional value. We have the flexibility to help you adjust quickly to changing conditions in the marketplace. And most important, we can strengthen your relationship with the key publics who support you — your clients, your customers, or your donors. That’s the benefit of design with muscle.
